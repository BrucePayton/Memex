# 10 Stock Trading

> Source: raw/10_stock_trading.md

This raw file contains 4 notes from Apple Notes covering stock trading strategies and data tools. The notes reference AKShare (version 1.18.7) documentation for stock data access. A FINNHUB_API_KEY environment variable export command is documented for market data integration. A "daiban" (打板) trading strategy is described: targeting stocks approaching the 10% daily price limit (涨停), buying in before or at the limit, position splitting, and selecting consecutive-limit stocks based on market sentiment and main theme clarity. Another note outlines a trading approach: monitoring for substantive positive sentiment, sectors at trend lows, intraday selecting the fastest-rising stocks with sector带动作用, buying at limit-up, and selling the next day if the limit opens or does not reach the limit.
